  • Personal Connect in an Online World February 22, 2021: Session presentation for the University of Manitoba Centre for the Advancement of Teaching and Learning Teaching Cafe 2021: A presentation of tools for fostering communication and personal connections with students when teaching in a remote environment.
  • Opening Lines of Dialogue: Facilitating Questions and Discussion in Small and Large Classrooms October 3, 2019: A seminar for the University of Manitoba Department of Statistics seminar series on ways to encourage discussions and communication between students with themselves and their professors.
  • Supplementing Class Content with Video: Addressing Student Needs and Accessibility May 7, 2019: Session presentation for the Univeristy of Manitoba Centre for the Advancement of Teaching and Learning Teaching Cafe 2019: Breakout session on using iPad video software (in particular Educreations) to create videos to supplement instructional material and the various ways they can be used for the classroom.
  • Active Learning in Labs and the classroom November 2018: Invited Talk for the Reg Litz Teaching Day 2018 for the Asper School of Business: A shorter presentation on active learning labs and planning them using the BOPPS lesson planning model.
  • Creating Student Centered Activities in the Lab and Classroom: BOPPPS and the Creation of STAT 1150 November 8, 2018: Department seminar talk for the University of Manitoba Department of Statistics. This talk was about creating active learning labs for introductory statistics and the use of the BOPPPS model for lesson planning the labs.
